Core Idea

What the agent
actually does.

Built for the messy reality of spreadsheet-driven operations.

Smart Column Mapping

Automatically detects and maps messy column headers to a canonical schema. Handles typos, abbreviations, and non-standard naming.

Structured Patch Sets

Every change is a reviewable patch with before/after values, risk levels, confidence scores, and human-readable reasoning.

Approval-First Flow

Nothing writes to your sheet without explicit approval. Bulk-approve low-risk, review high-risk individually.

AI Review Intelligence

The agent explains its reasoning, flags anomalies, and provides risk-aware summaries. Transparent, auditable, and actionable.
